Meet the Mediterranean Cats

When you think of the Mediterranean, you may picture clear blue waters, delicious cuisine, and historic architecture. But did you know that the region is also home to a unique breed of cats? The Mediterranean cat, also known as the "street cat," can be found throughout the region, from the shores of Spain to the hills of Greece.
These felines are also known for their friendly and sociable nature, often seen lounging in cafes or begging for scraps at outdoor restaurants. They have become an iconic part of the Mediterranean landscape, and their presence adds to the region's charm.
If you're traveling to the Mediterranean, keep an eye out for these adorable cats. You may just find yourself charmed by their playful antics and sweet purrs.
